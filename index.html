About Us

About HyLyf:

HyLyf hospital is an institution that is built, staffed, and equipped for the diagnosis of disease; for the treatment, both medical and surgical, of the sick and the injured; and for their housing during this process. Our modern Hylyf hospital also often serves as a centre for investigation and for teaching for the students to gain knowledge.

History:

The HyLyf Hospital was first founded by Crips Reddy.N in 1970 as the multi-speciality care provider clinic in Delhi,India.

Hospitality Features


OPERATION SUITE

Our new operating rooms, laboratory and Pathology department are equipped with the BDV IDSS OR Integration System.

Our new operating rooms, laboratory are equipped with the BDV IDSS OR Integration System. The technology enables providers to have up to 32 imaging devices and 32 display destinations for high definition viewing, capturing and recording. The system also connects teams within and beyond the OR for robust, real-time collaboration, communication, and education

PATIENT ROOM SUITE
